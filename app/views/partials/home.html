With Starfall, NTU students can find possible course schedules within few seconds. Here's how:

Step 1. Drag the Starfall bookmarklet above to your browser's bookmark bar.

Step 2. In STARS Planner, as usual, pick all courses that you want to try.

Step 3. Go back to STARS Planner's planning page.

Step 4. From that page, click on the bookmark you just created.

If you come up with something like this, you have done it right! Now you can always go back to step 2 and try more courses as you want. Good luck ;)
